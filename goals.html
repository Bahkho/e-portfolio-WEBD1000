As an IT programming student in Canada and aspiring frontend developer from Nigeria, here are five short-term goals (2 years) i am considering:

  1. Building a strong portfolio: Spend time building various frontend projects such as websites, web applications, or mobile apps that showcase your skills as a frontend developer. These projects will serve as evidence of your competence and be helpful when applying for jobs.
  2. Networking: Attend technology conferences, workshops, or meetups and connect with professionals in your field. This can help you learn about the latest trends and technologies, and potentially land job opportunities.
  3. Developing proficiency in relevant programming languages: As a frontend developer, you will need to be proficient in languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React and Angular. Set a goal to improve your skills in these areas through online courses, books, and practice.
  4. Internship: Try to secure an internship with a reputable company in Canada. This will provide you with valuable work experience, expose you to different work environments, and potentially lead to a full-time job after graduation.
  5. Working on soft skills: Soft skills such as communication, time management, and teamwork are essential in any profession, including IT. Practice and improve these skills by collaborating with classmates on group projects or participating in extracurricular activities.
